CS:GO Teams

Natus Vincere

Natus Vincere, often abbreviated as Na'Vi, is a Ukrainian esports organization founded in 2009. The team has had a strong presence in the CS:GO scene, with multiple championships and a reputation for their aggressive playstyle.

SK Gaming

SK Gaming is a German esports organization established in 1997. With a rich history in Counter-Strike, the team has seen multiple successful rosters over the years, earning numerous titles and solidifying their legacy in the competitive scene.

G2 Esports

G2 Esports is a Spanish esports organization founded in 2013. G2's CS:GO roster has experienced varying degrees of success, with a mix of international talent and a strong focus on team synergy and innovation.
