CS:GO Maps

Dust II

Image: Dust II Map's "A" Plant Site

Dust II, originally known as Dust 2, is one of the most iconic and popular maps in the Counter-Strike franchise. First introduced in 2001 as a sequel to the original Dust map, Dust II has been a staple in the game ever since.

Mirage

Image: Mirage Map's "A" Plant Site

Mirage is a competitive map set in a Middle Eastern city, and it was first introduced in 2013 for Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. The map is known for its tight angles and unique design, offering intense close-quarters combat and strategic gameplay. Mirage has two bomb sites: one inside a palace and the other in a courtyard.
