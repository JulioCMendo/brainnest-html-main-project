CS:GO Tournaments

CS:GO Major

CS Major, also known as Counter-Strike: Global Offensive Major Championships, is a series of premier tournaments held throughout the year, featuring the top teams from around the world. These events offer substantial prize pools and are organized by Valve, the developers of CS:GO. A Major win is considered one of the most prestigious achievements in the competitive scene.

IEM Katowice

Intel Extreme Masters (IEM) Katowice is an annual esports event held in Katowice, Poland. The latest tournament saw the G2 Esports roster take the trophy almost undefeated, having lost only a single map in their grand finals 3-1 win against Heroic.
